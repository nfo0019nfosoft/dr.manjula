Gynaecology Services

Menstrual Disorders Treatment

Diagnosis and modern treatments for irregular, heavy or painful menstrual cycles. We identify the root causes such as hormonal imbalance, thyroid issues, or uterine conditions. Based on the evaluation, personalized medical or minimally invasive treatments are provided. Our goal is to help women achieve comfortable, healthy, and regular menstrual cycles.

Irregular / Heavy Bleeding
Painful Periods

PCOS / PCOD Management

Holistic hormonal management, lifestyle guidance and fertility support for PCOS / PCOD. We perform detailed hormonal and ultrasound assessments to understand the condition clearly. Treatment focuses on restoring ovulation, regulating cycles, and improving long-term health. We also offer weight, skin, and fertility-related care for complete PCOS management.

Hormonal Evaluation
Lifestyle Guidance
Fertility Support

Thyroid & High Blood Pressure (Women)

Specialized monitoring and treatment tailored for women’s endocrine and blood pressure issues. Thyroid imbalance affects weight, mood, fertility, and menstrual patterns, requiring timely care. We offer accurate diagnosis and safe medication plans to stabilize hormone and BP levels. Regular follow-ups help prevent long-term health complications.

Thyroid Disorder Care
BP Monitoring

White Discharge Treatment

Accurate diagnosis and effective treatment of abnormal vaginal discharge and infections. We identify whether the discharge is due to fungal, bacterial, or hormonal causes. Treatment includes safe medications and preventive guidance to avoid recurrence. Early care ensures comfort, hygiene, and long-term vaginal health.

Uterine Fibroids

Diagnosis, medical options and minimally invasive procedures for fibroid management. Fibroids can cause heavy bleeding, abdominal pain, and fertility problems if untreated. We use ultrasound scanning and advanced treatment methods to manage them safely. Laparoscopic options ensure minimal pain and quicker recovery.

Ovarian Cyst Diagnosis & Treatment

Both medical and laparoscopic solutions are available for safe and effective treatment. We ensure long-term hormonal balance and symptom relief.
